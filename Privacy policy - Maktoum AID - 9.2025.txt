Privacy Policy - Maktoum AID
Last updated: 9/20/2025
Maktoum AID (we, our, or us), developed by Oummal under the Statelessness Program with technical support from E0, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the Maktoum AID mobile and web applications (the App).
By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information depending on your role within the App (Guest, Civil Society Organization/Activist/Academic, or Stateless Individual):
* Personal Information (Stateless Users):
Name, contact details, demographic information, case details (for those receiving legal support), survey responses, and any information voluntarily shared in the Hub.
* Organization/Activist Information:
Profile details, project descriptions, articles, events, polls, and surveys you choose to share.
* Guest Users:
Limited interaction data (e.g., country of interest, pages visited).
* Device & Technical Information:
Log data such as device type, operating system, IP address, and basic analytics to improve performance.

2. How We Use Information
We use collected data for the following purposes:
* To enable registration and profile creation for stateless individuals and organizations.
* To provide case tracking and support services.
* To send notifications, updates, and relevant information.
* To facilitate surveys, polls, and networking between users.
* To generate anonymized, aggregated data for research, advocacy, and policy influence.
* To maintain and improve the security, functionality, and user experience of the App.

3. How We Share Information
We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties. Information may only be shared as follows:
* With Civil Society Organizations or Legal Support Providers  when stateless individuals request support.
* With Partner Organizations and Researchers  in anonymized or aggregated form, for analysis and reporting.
* For Legal Reasons  if required by law, regulation, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users.

4. Data Storage and Security
* All data is stored on secure servers with encryption and industry-standard security practices.
* Regular code audits are conducted by independent parties to ensure data protection.
* Access to sensitive information is strictly limited to authorized personnel.

5. User Rights
Depending on your role and jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
* Access, review, and update your information.
* Request deletion of your account and personal data.
* Opt-out of receiving notifications or communications.
* Withdraw consent for data processing at any time (subject to legal obligations).
To exercise these rights, please contact us at: oummalorg@gmail.com.

6. Childrens Privacy
Maktoum AID is not designed for children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable by law). We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental or guardian consent. If we learn that a child has registered without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete their data promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published within the App and on our website, with the Last Updated date revised accordingly. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.
